Meat Loaf

Description:

An air fryer meat load is a pretty simple and quick meal for the whole family

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Finely chopped onion
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Mushrooms
  • Salt, pepper, Thyme, olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat airfryer to 392 F
  2. Combine Beef, onion, bread crumbs, thyme, egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Mix throrougly.Transfer to loaf pan. Put onions and olive oil on top
  3. Roast meatloaf in airfryer for about 25 minutes.
