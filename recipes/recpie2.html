Coconut Shrimp

Description:

Yummy Coconut shrimp! Perfect for all you sea fod lovers!

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • Flaked Coconut
  • Bread crumbs
  • Shrimp
  • Salt
  • Honey
  • Lime Juice

Steps

  1. Stir together flour and seasonings in a shallow dish.Lightly beat eggs in another dish. Stir together Coconut and bread crumbs in a thrid dish
  2. Throw shrimp into flour mixture, then egg, then bread mix.
  3. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F, put in shrimp for 3 minutes, turn, and then 3 more minutes.
  4. Combine honey and lime juice while shrimp is cooking, sprinkle on top when shrimp is done.
